Generate subtitled videos in 3 steps

Step 1

Transcription

Select your video file and let the Whisper model transcribe the audio automatically. Multi-track videos are supported, so you can choose which audio track to subtitle.

Step 2

Editing

Review and refine the transcribed text and timing directly on screen. Add, delete, or adjust line timing with an intuitive interface. Export your edits as plain text or SRT files for use in other video editing software.

Step 3

Export

Burn your edited text directly into the video. Customize text color, outline, and fade effects to your liking. From transcription to editing to export — everything is completed within this app, entirely on your PC.

Works with Short-form Videos

Subtitle vertical videos with ease

Supports vertical videos (9:16). Import various formats including MKV, MP4, MOV (iPhone videos), and AVI, then export as MP4 for direct uploading to TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Instagram Reels.
